Pocketdudes

How to Play

The game is a turn-based monster fighting game. So each turn you select a move, and then both monsters (pocketdudes) perform their move. To win, you must defeat three of the opponent's pocketdudes. You are given three random pocketdudes.

The stats are named weirdly (as a joke) and the following are the correlation to the stats:

  • Health ⇒ Swag
  • Attack ⇒ Hustle
  • Defense ⇒ Thiccness
  • Special Attack ⇒ Pimpness
  • Special Defense ⇒ Awesomeness
  • Speed ⇒ Quickness
